ARKA JAIN University is one of the leading state private Universities in the Eastern region of the country and the first state private University in the entire Kolhan Region comprising of the three districts of East Singhbhum, West Singhbhum and Saraikela-Kharsawan. The only NAAC ‘A’ accredited state private university in Bihar, Jharkhand and West Bengal in the first cycle, AJU has carved a niche for itself. Today, AJU is the fastest-growing state private university in the state of Jharkhand. The University was established by the JHARKHAND State Legislature under “The ARKA JAIN University Act” in the year 2017. It is recognized by UGC, AICTE, BCI, PCI, ASCO, OCI, INC, JNRC (for relevant programmes) and other statutory bodies and government agencies as applicable. An illustrious 13-year legacy characterizes the success of the University (prior to becoming a state private university, JAIN College provided quality higher education since 2011) through a gamut of diploma, undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes. The University is part of the illustrious JAIN Group, Bengaluru and mentored by JAIN (Deemed-to-Be-University), Bengaluru, a NAAC A++ and NIRF Top-100 HEI. AJU is an ISO Certified Standard EOMS 21001:2018 University and is a member of Association of Indian Universities (AIU). The University has been ranked 3rd in the “Top-15 Emerging Private Universities in India” category in the prestigious Outlook-ICARE India’s Top Universities Ranking 2023.
